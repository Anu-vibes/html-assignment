Special Characters Using HTML Entities

Here are some examples of special characters:
Ampersand: & (&amp;)
Less than: < (&lt;) and Greater than: > (&gt;)
Copyright: © (&copy;)
Registered trademark: ® (&reg;)
Trademark: ™ (&trade;)
